OUR STORY

Built by a Parent.
For Children Who Need a Voice.

XpressAble didn't start in a lab or a startup accelerator. It started at home — with a parent who wanted to help his son find the right words.

How XpressAble Began

My son sometimes struggles to find the right words in conversation. Not because he doesn't have thoughts — he has plenty — but because in the moment, when someone asks him a question, the words don't always come. I watched him freeze, look away, or give a one-word answer that didn't capture what he really meant.

As a parent, it's one of the hardest things to watch. You know there's so much inside, and you just wish you could give him a little nudge — not answer for him, but help him get there himself.

"I wanted something that gives him the words he needs, right when he needs them — a gentle nudge, not a replacement for his voice."

I started with iPhone Shortcuts, trying to build a simple workflow that could listen to a question and whisper a suggested response through his headphones. It worked — imperfectly, but it worked enough to know we were onto something.

That prototype became XpressAble. A proper iOS app, powered by AI, designed from the ground up with one goal: to help children who can speak, but sometimes need a little help finding their words, communicate more naturally and confidently.

XpressAble is free. It always will be during this period of growth. Because every child who needs this deserves access to it — not just families who can afford a subscription.

Our Mission & Values

Everything we build is guided by these principles.

🌱

A Nudge, Not a Crutch

XpressAble is designed to support communication, not replace it. We believe every child has a voice — we just help them find it in the moment.

💙

Built with Empathy

Every feature is designed by someone who understands this challenge personally. We build what we wish had existed — with the community's needs at the centre.

🔓

Free to Access

Assistive technology should not be a luxury. XpressAble is free during beta, and we are committed to keeping it accessible to as many families as possible.

🌍

Inclusive by Design

Supporting English, Spanish, French, and Hindi — because communication challenges don't follow language boundaries, and neither should XpressAble.

🤝

Community Shaped

Our beta testers, therapists, and families guide every update. The feedback loop isn't just a feature — it's how the whole app improves.

🔒

Privacy First

We handle your child's data with the utmost care. No data is sold. No advertising. XpressAble exists to help, not to profit from your family's information.

Who XpressAble Is For

🧒

Children with Autism Spectrum Disorder

For children on the autism spectrum who are verbal but benefit from prompting and support in real-time conversations and social situations.

💬

Children with Speech & Language Delays

For children who have the thoughts but struggle to articulate them quickly — giving them words in the moment builds confidence over time.

😰

Children with Social Anxiety

For children who know what they want to say but freeze in social situations — a quiet prompt through headphones can be the bridge they need.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parents, Caregivers & Educators

For the people who support these children every day — XpressAble is a tool to have on hand in classrooms, therapy sessions, family dinners, and daily life.
