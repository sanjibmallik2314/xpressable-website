How It Works

Three simple steps to give your child their voice.

1

Set up a Profile

Enter your child's name, age, interests, and communication preferences. XpressAble uses this to make every response feel natural and personal.

2

Tap to Listen

When someone asks your child a question, tap the big button. XpressAble listens, transcribes the question, and generates a response in seconds.

3

Hear and Respond

The response is spoken aloud through the headphones. Your child hears what to say — a gentle nudge that builds confidence one conversation at a time.

4

Review and Improve

Check Chat History to see all exchanges. Rate responses and suggest better ones — the AI gets smarter about your child's specific needs over time.
