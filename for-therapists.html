FOR SPEECH-LANGUAGE PATHOLOGISTS & THERAPISTS

A Free AAC Tool You Can Recommend with Confidence

XpressAble supports children with autism, speech delays, and communication challenges between therapy sessions — giving them real-time prompting in everyday conversations.

What Is XpressAble?

XpressAble is a free iOS app that listens to questions directed at a child, generates a warm, age-appropriate first-person response, and speaks it aloud through the child's headphones. The child hears what to say — a gentle prompt that helps them participate in conversation more naturally.

It works as a complement to speech therapy — not a replacement for it. Think of it as a real-time communication support tool that families can use at the dinner table, in the classroom, during social events, and in everyday conversations.

Designed as a nudge, not a crutch. XpressAble gives children the words in the moment, building conversational confidence over time. Responses are spoken privately through the child's headphones — discreet, immediate, and tailored to each child's profile.

XpressAble is free to download and use during the beta period. There is no cost to families you recommend it to, and no sign-up required to try the interactive demo at xpressable.app.

How XpressAble Supports Your Clients

Designed to work alongside therapy goals, not in opposition to them.

🔁

Bridges the Gap Between Sessions

Therapy happens once or twice a week. Conversations happen every day. XpressAble gives children real-time support in the moments between sessions where they need it most.

🎯

First-Person Response Framing

Every response is generated in the child's voice — "I feel...", "I think...", "I want..." — reinforcing the language patterns and communication styles therapists work toward in sessions.

👤

Personalised to Each Child

Parents set up a profile with the child's name, age, interests, and communication style. Responses feel natural and personal — not generic.

📋

Chat History for Review

Every exchange is logged. Therapists and parents can review what questions came up and how the child responded — useful data for tracking progress and identifying patterns.

🌍

Multilingual Support

Supports English, Spanish, French, and Hindi — making it accessible to diverse families, including those working with bilingual children.

🎧

Discreet Headphone Delivery

Responses are spoken privately through the child's headphones — so support is invisible to others and doesn't draw attention in social situations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is XpressAble a replacement for speech therapy?

Absolutely not — and it's not designed to be. XpressAble is a support tool for use in daily life, between therapy sessions. It works best as a complement to professional speech-language therapy, not a substitute for it.

What devices does it run on?

XpressAble is currently available on iPhone (iOS). It works best with headphones — bone-conduction headphones like the Shokz OpenRun are particularly well-suited as they allow the child to hear the response and the world around them simultaneously.

Is it free for families?

Yes — XpressAble is completely free during the beta period. Families can download it via TestFlight with no cost, no subscription, and no credit card required.

What age group is it suitable for?

XpressAble is designed primarily for school-age children, though the profile settings can be adjusted for different ages and communication levels. We recommend trying the interactive demo at xpressable.app to assess suitability for specific clients.

How do I recommend it to a family?

Simply share the link xpressable.app — families can try the interactive demo in their browser instantly, then download via the TestFlight link on the page. No account or sign-up needed to explore it first.
